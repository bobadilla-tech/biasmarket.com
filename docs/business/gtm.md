# 📘 Bias Market — GTM Playbook (Outbound & Activation)

## 🎯 Mission

Your job is simple:

> **Find K-pop GOMs → contact them → get them to try Bias Market → help them launch their first drop**

Everything you do should push this forward.

---

# 🧠 What is a GOM?

A **GOM (Group Order Manager)** is someone who:

- Organizes group orders for albums, photocards, or merch
- Uses:
  - Google Forms
  - Google Sheets
  - Instagram DMs

- Manually tracks payments and buyers

👉 These are your target users.

---

# 🔍 Step 1 — Find Leads

## Where to look

- Instagram
- Twitter

## What to search

- “GO open”
- “group order”
- “photocard sale”
- “slots open”
- “wts photocard”

## What a good lead looks like

- Actively selling (recent posts)
- Mentions group orders or slots
- Has buyers commenting or engaging

## What to record

- Username
- Profile link
- What they sell (albums, photocards, etc.)

---

# 💬 Step 2 — Outreach

## Goal

Start conversations with potential users.

## Message to send

hey! saw your group order 👀

quick question — are you still using forms + sheets + dms to manage everything?

i'm working on something that replaces all of that
(buyers order + upload payment in one place)

i can set it up for your next drop for free if you want to try 🙌

## Rules

- Keep it short
- Do not send links in the first message
- Be casual and friendly
- Do not sound like a company

---

# 🔁 Step 3 — Follow Up

If they don’t reply, send:

hey! just bumping this in case you missed it 🙌

i can set up your next GO so you don’t deal with spreadsheets anymore

---

# 🎯 Step 4 — Qualify & Understand

When they reply:

## Ask simple questions

- “how do you manage your orders right now?”
- “how many buyers do you usually have?”

## What you’re looking for

- Signs of pain (confusion, too many messages, manual work)
- Active sellers (they run drops regularly)

---

# 🛠️ Step 5 — Get Them to Try

## Goal

Move them from “interested” → “let’s try it”

## What to say

- Offer to **set everything up for them**
- Emphasize:
  - Less chaos
  - No spreadsheets
  - Easier payment tracking

---

# 🚀 Step 6 — Activation

A user is successful when:

> **They launch their first drop using Bias Market**

## Your role

- Help them get started
- Answer questions
- Encourage them to use it for their next drop

---

# 📊 Tracking

Keep everything in a simple sheet:

- Leads found
- Messages sent
- Replies
- Interested users
- Activated users

---

# 📈 Weekly Targets

You are expected to hit:

- **200–300 leads found**
- **200–300 messages sent**
- **20–40 replies**
- **5–10 activated users (stores live)**

---

# 🔥 Prioritization

Focus more on:

- Active sellers
- People already doing group orders
- Accounts with engaged buyers

Focus less on:

- Inactive accounts
- People not currently selling

---

totally fair — most people i talk to say the same at first 😅

happy to set it up for you to test on one drop if you want

yeah for now i’m setting it up for early users for free

main goal is helping you run smoother drops

nice 🙌

it’s basically:

---

# 💣 Key Principle

> **Consistency beats everything**

Even if responses are low at first:

- Keep finding leads
- Keep sending messages
- Keep following up

That’s how you win.
