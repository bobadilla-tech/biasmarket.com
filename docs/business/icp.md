# BarriStore — Ideal Customer Profile (First 10 Users)

## 🎯 Core ICP

**K-pop Group Order Leaders (GOMs)** who are currently running sales manually
using:

- Google Forms
- Spreadsheets
- Instagram / Twitter (X) DMs
- Telegram / WhatsApp

---

## 👤 Persona: “The GOM Operator”

### Profile

- Age: 16–30
- Location: LATAM, Southeast Asia, US (but LATAM is your easiest entry)
- Runs **fan-led group orders** for:
  - Albums
  - Photocards
  - Limited merch

---

## 🔥 Behavioral Traits (THIS is what matters)

They:

- Run **repeated drops** (not one-time sellers)
- Handle **10–200+ buyers per drop**
- Post frequently on:
  - Instagram
  - Twitter (X)

- Already have:
  - A small but loyal audience

---

## ⚠️ Current Workflow (Pain Points)

Their current stack:

```txt
Google Form → collects orders
Spreadsheet → tracks payments
DMs → confirm payments
Manual review → screenshots
```

### Pain:

- Losing track of payments
- Screenshot chaos
- Constant “did you receive my payment?” messages
- Hard to scale beyond ~50 orders

---

## 💸 Buying Signal (CRITICAL)

Your ICP is NOT “any fan seller”.

They MUST:

- Already handling **real volume**
- Already feeling **operational pain**
- Already spending **time, not money**

---

## 🚫 Who is NOT your ICP

Avoid:

- Casual sellers (1–5 orders)
- People just “testing”
- People who never sold anything
- People asking for “free forever”

---

## 💥 Ideal First 10 Users (Exact Definition)

Find users who:

- Run **at least 1 group order per month**
- Handle **30–100+ orders**
- Use **manual payment methods**
- Actively post selling content
- Complain (publicly or privately) about:
  - tracking orders
  - payment confusion
  - admin stress

---

## 🧲 Where to Find Them

### Instagram

Search:

- “GO manager”
- “group order”
- “[group name] GO”
- “photocard GO”

Look for:

- highlight stories
- order forms in bio

---

### Twitter (X)

Search:

- “GO open”
- “group order open”
- “[group] photocard sale”

---

### Telegram / Discord

- K-pop trading groups
- Fanbase servers

---

## 🧪 Validation Script (How to Approach Them)

DM them:

```txt
Hey — quick question

I saw you're running group orders and handling payments manually.

I'm building a tool specifically for GOMs to:
- track orders
- collect proof of payment
- reduce DM chaos

Would you be open to trying it for your next drop?

Happy to set it up for you personally.
```

---

## 🎯 Your Goal with First 10 Users

NOT scale.

You want:

- Deep feedback
- Real usage
- Pain confirmation

---

## 🧠 What Success Looks Like

For your first 10 users:

- They use it for **real orders**
- They stop using Google Forms
- They say: → “this saved me time”

---

## 🔥 Hidden Insight

Your real ICP is:

> “People already doing business inefficiently”

Not:

> “People who want to start a store”

---

## 🧭 Expansion Later

After K-pop GOMs:

- Anime merch sellers
- TikTok small creators
- Instagram boutique sellers

---

## 🧨 Final Rule

If they don’t already feel pain…

They will NOT pay.
